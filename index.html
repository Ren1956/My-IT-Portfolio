About Me

I am an IT student currently studying in Batanagas State University. I am 18 yeears old, and this webpage is dedicated for SDG number 14 life below water. What is SDG 14? well this SDG focuses on the importance of the sea, specifically the many lives and eco system it contains. As The ocean alone is essential for human life and the planet, providing food, medicines, biofuels, and more. It also helps remove waste and pollution, reduce storm damage, and act as a carbon sink. Meanwhile the life it houses are just as important as without them, we'd have less food, less money, and less life on our planet. The bottom line is, life on Earth needs healthy oceans, and as someone who personally lives nearby the sea and has seen and experienced the beauty of the sea, it's extra important for me to stand by and support marine life, and their home. as intelligent beings it's our responsibility to protect them.

Pollution Below Water/A VR Experience

Pollution Below Water is a virtual reality escape room based on the UN's SDG #14: Life Under Water. This is a very early prototype that has no win condition, menu, and tutorial implemented. Hold the face button to teleport. Use the grip button to grab stuff. Use the side grip button to call objects to your hand by pointing it at them. (By SlaggyWolfie)

Life Below Water

Concept Note: Life Below Water The concept focuses on protecting life below water by reducing marine pollution, protecting marine ecosystems, regulating fishing, and conserving marine areas. It emphasizes reducing waste and nutrient pollution, restoring ecosystems, managing coastal areas sustainably, preventing overfishing, and expanding marine protected areas with international cooperation. (By Kasturi-Dey)
